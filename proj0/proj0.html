Part 1: Selfie - The Wrong Way vs. The Right Way

Ine the pictures on the left, the camera is held close to my face, so parts of my face close to the camera, like my nose and mouth, appear larger, compared to the parts further away from the camera, like the size of my face and ears. As the camera moves further from my face, the distance from the different parts of my face to the camera evens out, giving a much more realistic and accurate impression of my face.
